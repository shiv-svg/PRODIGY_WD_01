Hackernox Safety Rules and Regulations

  • Treat all attendees, speakers, and staff with respect and courtesy
  • Harassment or disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated
  • Be on time for all sessions, workshops, and events
  • Use electronic devices discreetly to avoid disturbing others
  • Respect the privacy and preferences of others regarding photography.
  • Dress appropriately for the professional and networking nature of the event.
  • Engage in networking with a positive and professional demeanor.
  • Business cards and promotional materials should be exchanged respectfully.
  • During Q&A sessions, wait your turn to ask questions.
  • Keep questions concise and relevant to the session topic.
  • Minimize disruptions during sessions.
  • Side conversations should be taken outside the session room.
  • Avoid leaving and entering session rooms frequently.
  • Unauthorized solicitation or distribution of promotional materials is not permitted.
  • Respect the designated spaces for promotional activities.
  • Attendees must comply with all local laws and regulations.
  • Conference organizers will cooperate with authorities if any legal issues arise.
